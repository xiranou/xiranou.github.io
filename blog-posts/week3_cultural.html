Thinking Style

Week-3 Cultural Blog 08/21/2014

According to the test, my thinking style is Abstract Sequential. That is such a foreign word. Not only the wording is foreign to me, but it is also a new concept I've come across. I've never pay much attention to my thinking style, let alone knowing which one I fit in. I always thought the way I think is just simply following the logic of things. I refuse anything that seems illogical, any argument that lacks a solid logical support will be not be accepted into my brain. I love arguments that present solid supporting documents and strict logical reasonings. When it comes to learning, to grasp upon a subject, I'll need to have detailed rational references. Most of the time they will come from my own experimenting or practicing on smaller pieces of such matter. After I did more research on my thinking style, the more I understand how I learn and why do I learn in such manner.
